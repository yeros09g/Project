They both die at the end

ADAM SILVERA

2012

Summary

In the end they both die is a youth literature novel written by American author Adam Silvera and published on September 5, 2017, by HarperTeen. It is Silvera's third novel focused on two teenage boys, Mateo and Rufus, who discover that they only have one day to live. Meanwhile, a terrible villain has escaped from Azkaban prison, Sirius Black, a serial killer with magical powers who was an accomplice of Lord Voldemort and who seems willing to eliminate Harry from the map. And if this were not enough, Harry must also face some terrible monsters, the Dementors, abominable beings capable of stealing the happiness of wizards and erasing all beautiful memories of those who dare to look at them.

Harry Potter

Harry is impatiently awaiting the start of his third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. After turning thirteen, alone and far from his Hogwarts friends, Harry gets into a fight with his mustachioed Aunt Marge, whom he turns into a balloon, and must escape on a magical bus. Meanwhile, a terrible villain has escaped from Azkaban prison, Sirius Black, a serial killer with magical powers who was an accomplice of Lord Voldemort and who seems willing to eliminate Harry from the map. And if this were not enough, Harry must also face some terrible monsters, the Dementors, abominable beings capable of stealing the happiness of wizards and erasing all beautiful memories of those who dare to look at them.

The Complete Persepolis

Persepolis is the story of Satrapi's unforgettable childhood and coming of age within a large and loving family in Tehran during the Islamic Revolution; of the contradictions between private life and public life in a country plagued by political upheaval; of her high school years in Vienna facing the trials of adolescence far from her family; of her homecoming—both sweet and terrible; and, finally, of her self-imposed exile from her beloved homeland. It is the chronicle of a girlhood and adolescence at once outrageous and familiar, a young life entwined with the history of her country yet filled with the universal trials and joys of growing up.

A Big Day's Knigth

Five year old JJ Nowesh lives in a magic kingdom. It totally stinks. Not because of magic, or the kingdom, but because his Mom brings new dimensions to the word “mean.” But when JJ sneaks into the Court Wizard’s lab to magic his Mom nice, he accidentally BIG-ifies himself and his best friend Frilly. Can they save the day AND not get grounded for eternity? A laugh out loud fantasy adventure perfect for all ages.

They both die at the end

In the end they both die is a youth literature novel written by American author Adam Silvera and published on September 5, 2017, by HarperTeen. It is Silvera's third novel focused on two teenage boys, Mateo and Rufus, who discover that they only have one day to live.
